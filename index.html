Digitalpath Innovations
e-commerce Analysis

A digital marketing company focused on optimizing user experience, I analyzed an A/B test conducted by DigitalPath Innovations using Tableau. The project aimed to determine which webpage version—Version A or Version B—performed better across engagement metrics like time on site, page views, actions taken, and conversion rates. I also evaluated user feedback submissions to gauge satisfaction and engagement levels with each version. The analysis also included assessing content interaction, particularly with video elements, and understanding the influence of different referral sources on user preferences. By delving into user interactions and feedback, I identified insights to inform future design iterations and enhance overall user satisfaction and engagement on the website.
